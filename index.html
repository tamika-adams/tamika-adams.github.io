Producer / Reporter / Writer

Tamika Adams is a freelance podcast producer and audio reporter based in Los Angeles, California. She's currently a producer with Dustlight Productions. Her latest project is LAist Studio's California Love podcast. She has produced for KPCC's daily magazine show Take Two and contributed to KPCC's web arm LAist.com. Tamika is also working to develop a podcast which centers on the complex process of grief - more soon.
